What this is

A competition system, not a server website

Unified Events runs multi-community Minecraft civilization seasons on a fixed structure: Discord identity for every account, defined phases for every season, and player-driven diplomacy, economy, and conflict that gets ruled on rather than improvised.

Discord-native identity

Every account, application, and result ties back to one Discord ID. No separate registration, no duplicate accounts.

Season-based structure

Each season runs through defined phases with a fixed start, a fixed end, and an archived outcome.

Player-driven systems

Diplomacy, economy, and conflict are decided by participants, then logged and ruled on through the platform.

Built for scale

Multiple communities, civilizations, and staff teams operate inside one shared event system.

How it works

From sign-in to season archive

Four fixed stages take every account from registration to a recorded result.

01

Sign in with Discord

Your Discord identity becomes your platform account — no separate registration step.

02

Apply to a season

Choose a role — civilization leader, participant, solo competitor, or staff — and submit before the season opens.

03

Compete through structured phases

Each season moves through building, diplomacy, conflict, and resolution on a fixed timeline.

04

Result goes to the archive

Outcomes are recorded permanently and tied to your account history.
