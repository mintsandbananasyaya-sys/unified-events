Unified Events uses Discord as its identity layer. Every account, application, and seasonal result is tied to a single Discord ID. There is no separate registration — signing in with Discord is the only step required.

In the event of a technical disruption such as a server crash or world instability, Unified Events administrators review logs to determine whether a rollback or corrective restoration is required. Any affected progress is reconstructed in accordance with the closest verifiable state recorded within the archive.

## How do i Apply

To apply for Season 1, go to https://unified-apply.onrender.com and log in with your Discord account. You must be a member of this server. Fill out the 5-section application form (Information, Minecraft Experience, Event Questions, Final Checks) and submit. Staff will review your application within 24–72 hours.

## How Long do Applications Take

Staff aim to review applications within **24–72 hours**. You'll receive a Discord DM from the bot when a decision has been made. You can also check your status on your dashboard at https://unified-events.onrender.com/dashboard or by running **/status** in the server.

## Application Attempts

You have a maximum of **2 application attempts per season**. If your first application is denied, staff may grant you a second attempt. If your second application is also denied, you won't be able to apply again until a new season opens. You'll be notified via DM for every decision.

<!-- aliases: `application attempts`, `how many times can i apply`, `second chance`, `reapply`, `apply twice`, `two attempts` -->

<!-- source: brain2.js; id: brain2-8 -->

## Denied

If your application was denied, you'll receive a DM from the bot explaining the outcome. Staff may include a note. If it was your first attempt, staff might grant you a second chance — watch your DMs and notifications. If it was your second attempt, you'll need to wait until a new season opens to apply again.

• **Staff** — General moderation and management
• **Application Reviewer** — Can review, accept, and reject Season 1 applications

## Application Not Saving

If you're having trouble submitting your application:
1. Make sure all required fields are filled in — look for any red error messages.
2. Make sure you're logged in with Discord before starting the form.
3. Try refreshing and starting the form again — your session should persist.
4. If the issue continues, open a ticket via **/forms**.

Roles in Unified Events:
