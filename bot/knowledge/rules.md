## Staff

Unified Events staff operate as a neutral governing body across all six civilization communities, responsible for ruling on disputes, moderating seasonal conduct, and maintaining the integrity of recorded outcomes. They are not affiliated with any single Discord server.

## Application Form

The application form has 5 sections:
**Section A — Information:** Discord username, Minecraft IGN, age, timezone, country, available hours, voice chat, recordings.
**Section B — Minecraft Experience:** Years played, Java/Bedrock, player type, PvP skill (1–5), building skill (1–5).
**Section C — Event Questions:** Why you want to join, what you plan to do, what makes you fit, preferred spawn country in Europe.
**Section D — Final Checks:** Inactivity agreements, commitment level (1–5), rules acknowledgement, anything else.

## Rules

Key points: respect all players, no cheating or exploiting, inactivity may result in removal, and consistent inactivity may affect your eligibility for future events.
